What is FIRST and FRC?

The most difficult fun you will ever have...

FIRST is a global non-profit organization dedicated to inspiring our youth to pursue careers in science and technology. FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is one of the programs offered by FIRST, specifically designed for high school students to design, build, and compete with robots in a sports-like environment. First Robotics Competition is more than just building robots; students are tested every day of the season to become the best version of themselves through STEM and competition.

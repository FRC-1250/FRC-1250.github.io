Why your support matters

Running a competitive robotics team costs over $15,000 per season. That money goes to things such as:

  • Materials and parts
  • Competition entry fees
  • Travel expenses
  • Team uniforms and safety equipment

How you can help

By sponsoring the Gator-Bots, you invest directly in the future of STEM education in our community. We offer a variety of recognition opportunities, including:

  • Logo placement on banners, robot, and uniforms
  • Social media (Instagram) and website features
  • Custom thank-you packages
  • Exclusive demonstrations
